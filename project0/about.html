About Me

I am Denis Pointer, CCIE #59241. Currently I work as a Solution Architect in a professional services capacity for a Cisco partner and service provider. I have worked in this group since 2006 in a variety of roles. These roles have included design, implementation and support. My primarily focus is the Cisco Collaboration portfolio, with a secondary focus on enterprise networking.

I achieved my first Cisco certification (CCNA) in 2006. On May 22, 2018 I achieved a CCIE certification in Routing & Switching. Currently I hold several certifications including:

  • CCIE Routing and Switching #59241
  • CCNP Routing and Switching
  • CCNP Collaboration
  • CCDP
  • CCNA Routing and Switching
  • CCDA
